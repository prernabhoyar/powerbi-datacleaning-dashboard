DATA CLEANING & REPORTING AUTOMATION - INSIGHTS

1. Data Cleaning Process
- Imported the Superstore dataset into Power BI.
- Removed duplicate records to improve data quality.
- Applied text cleaning and trimming to standardize textual data.
- Verified and corrected data types for numerical and date fields.
- Improved dataset consistency for reporting and analysis.

2. Key Dashboard Metrics
- Total Sales: 2.30M
- Total Profit: 286.40K

3. Sales Analysis
- Technology category generated the highest sales among all categories.
- Furniture and Office Supplies also contributed significantly to overall revenue.
- Sales were distributed across all regions, with the West region contributing the largest share.

4. Regional Performance
- West region recorded the highest sales.
- East region showed strong sales performance.
- Central and South regions contributed moderate sales volumes.

5. Trend Analysis
- Sales trends varied over time based on order dates.
- The dashboard helps identify sales patterns and performance fluctuations.

6. Business Benefits
- Automated data cleaning reduces manual effort and errors.
- Interactive dashboards provide quick access to business insights.
- Improved reporting efficiency supports faster decision-making.
- Data preprocessing ensures more reliable and accurate analysis.

Conclusion:
This project demonstrates how Power BI can be used to automate data cleaning and reporting workflows. The resulting dashboard provides clear insights into sales performance, profit generation, category-wise contribution, regional distribution, and sales trends, enabling efficient data-driven decision-making.
